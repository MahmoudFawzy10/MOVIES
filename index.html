Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson

Dwayne Johnson is an American actor, producer, and former professional wrestler. Known for his charismatic personality and action-packed roles, he has starred in blockbuster movies such as *Fast & Furious*, *Jumanji*, and *Black Adam*.

Born: May 2, 1972

Nationality: American

Notable Films: Fast & Furious, Jumanji, Black Adam

Chadwick Boseman

Chadwick Boseman was an American actor known for his legendary role as *T’Challa / Black Panther* in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. His performances in *42*, *Get on Up*, and *Ma Rainey’s Black Bottom* showcased his incredible talent and versatility.

Born: November 29, 1976

Died: August 28, 2020

Notable Films: Black Panther, 42, Ma Rainey's Black Bottom

Leonardo DiCaprio

Leonardo DiCaprio is an award-winning actor, environmentalist, and film producer. He gained worldwide fame for playing *Jack Dawson* in *Titanic* and has since starred in critically acclaimed films like *Inception*, *The Wolf of Wall Street*, and *The Revenant*.

Born: November 11, 1974

Nationality: American

Notable Films: Titanic, Inception, The Wolf of Wall Street
